About

I hold a B.Sc. in Computer Science and Engineering from Shahjalal University of Science and Technology (SUST) and currently work as a full-time Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at Green University of Bangladesh in Dhaka.

With a background in AI engineering, my research focuses on multilingual speech technologies and the development of accessible language tools for underrepresented communities, particularly those involving regional and low-resource languages. My academic interests include applied machine learning, large language model (LLM) evaluation, and the intersection of ecological informatics with machine learning and speech technologies.

I aim to advance speech processing, natural language understanding (NLU), and text-to-speech (TTS) systems to develop accessible AI products that drive progress in technology, education, and conservation.
